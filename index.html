CAT HISTORY

Domesticated over 9,000 years ago, cats were revered by ancient Egyptians for pest control and symbolic significance. They spread globally, becoming beloved household pets, representing mystery, independence, and protection in various cultures.

CAT FOOD

Cat food should be high in animal protein, as cats are obligate carnivores. Quality food includes wet or dry options, providing essential nutrients like taurine, vitamins, and minerals for a balanced diet.

more
CAT CARE

Cats require a protein-rich diet, regular grooming, and a clean litter box. Provide fresh water, toys, and mental stimulation. Routine vet visits ensure their health, while indoor living protects from harm.
